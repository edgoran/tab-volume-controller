# Privacy Policy — Levels - Tab Volume Mixer

## Data Collection
Levels does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

## Storage
All settings (volume presets, preferences) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No data leaves your browser.

## Permissions
- **tabs**: Used to identify which tabs are playing audio
- **storage**: Used to save your volume preferences locally
- **scripting**: Used to inject the volume control script into web pages
- **host_permissions**: Required to control audio on any website
